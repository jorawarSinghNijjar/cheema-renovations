Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in the Greater Toronto Area do you serve?

We proudly serve the entire Greater Toronto Area, including but not limited to Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, and Vaughan.

What types of basement renovation services do you offer?

Our services include basement finishing, waterproofing, insulation, flooring installation, custom cabinetry, and more. We handle everything from design to construction.

How long does a typical basement renovation project take?

The duration of a project varies depending on the size and scope of the renovation. On average, a basement renovation can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks to complete.

Are your contractors licensed and insured?

Yes, all our contractors are fully licensed and insured. We prioritize safety and professionalism in every project we undertake.

Can I customize the design of my basement renovation?

Absolutely! We work closely with our clients to understand their vision and preferences. Our team will help you customize every aspect of your basement renovation to reflect your style.

Do you provide free estimates for basement renovation projects?

Yes, we offer free estimates for all potential projects. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and receive a detailed quote tailored to your specific requirements.

What sets Cheema Renovations apart from other contractors?

At Cheema Renovations, we combine quality craftsmanship with exceptional customer service. Our attention to detail, transparent communication, and commitment to client satisfaction set us apart from the rest.

How can I get started with my basement renovation project?

Getting started is easy! Simply reach out to us through our website or give us a call. We'll schedule an initial consultation to discuss your ideas, budget, and timeline for the project.
